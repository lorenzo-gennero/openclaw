- Layering is subtle — 2 elements that work together beat 10 elements that don't.

2. Low end — is the kick punchy but not boomy? Is the bass present but not fighting?
3. Does it have space? Is there room to breathe, or is it cluttered?
4. Would this play well at 2am in a dark room? Or does it feel like bedroom pop?
5. Is there a moment of tension and release, or does it just loop?
